About Me

I'm Rabbit Stoddard, and I do all the things, and also none of the things. I like chill, not writing bios, and you. Sometimes, I read tarot cards.

Dwelling in a silence which was both the perfected state, and the moment of catalyst for utmost perfidy. Sages warn constantly of the direst crimes engendered by silence, even as their voices shutter, their mouths stop up, and words cease flowing from their lips and dry to baked and abandoned antiquities-- and then are ultimately forgotten. So do gods and criminals both teeter in their respective isolations.
